Debt framing

The message tries to make disagreement feel like betrayal.

Why it works: it respects history without surrendering autonomy.

Important boundaries.

Measured is a conflict communication coach. It helps people slow down and respond better, but it does not replace emergency, legal, medical, or mental-health support.

Does Measured read WhatsApp directly?

No. Users manually import a supported text export. Measured does not connect to a WhatsApp account.

Are voice notes analyzed?

Not in the first release. The initial website and app copy stay honest: text-only export support first.

Is this therapy?

No. Measured gives communication coaching support. It is not therapy, legal advice, medical advice, or emergency support.

When does hosted AI run?

Only when configured, signed in, entitled, and within usage limits. Local analysis remains available as fallback.
