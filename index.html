Mind-reading demand

Urgency + loyalty test

The trap is accepting an unreasonable demand to avoid looking difficult.

I can prioritize this tomorrow morning. I can't commit to tonight without dropping existing work. If it's critical, tell me what should move.
Why it works: it gives options without volunteering burnout.

Debt framing

The message tries to make disagreement feel like betrayal.

I'm grateful for what you've done. I'm still allowed to make this decision differently. I'd like us to talk without turning it into a debt.
Why it works: it respects history without surrendering autonomy.

WhatsApp chat export

Users can upload a text-only WhatsApp export so ClearTone understands relationship history instead of judging one message in isolation.

  • Detect recurring patterns across months or years of text.
  • Separate one-off conflict from repeated pressure loops.
  • Build a private relationship brief for more personalized replies.

Built for trust

Imported chats are used to help the user understand their own relationship context, not to sell, expose, or publicly train on private conversations.

  • Local-first parsing whenever possible.
  • Clear consent before hosted coaching is used.
  • User-controlled deletion of feedback and memory.

Private by design.

ClearTone deals with intimate, emotionally loaded conversations. The product and the website lead with privacy, consent, and control: no hidden sharing, no public training on user chats, and no storage without a clear reason.

Important boundaries.

ClearTone is a conflict communication coach. It helps people slow down and respond better, but it does not replace emergency, legal, medical, or mental-health support.

Does ClearTone read WhatsApp directly?

No. Users manually import a supported text export. ClearTone does not connect to a WhatsApp account.

Are voice notes analyzed?

Not in the first release. The initial website and app copy stay honest: text-only export support first.

Is this therapy?

No. ClearTone gives communication coaching support. It is not therapy, legal advice, medical advice, or emergency support.

When does hosted AI run?

Only when configured, signed in, entitled, and within usage limits. Local analysis remains available as fallback.
