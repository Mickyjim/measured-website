Privacy policy draft

Privacy-first conflict coaching.

This page is ready as website structure, but the final legal wording and contact details should be reviewed before App Store submission.

Overview

ClearTone helps users understand difficult messages and prepare safer replies. The app is designed to keep the core experience local-first where possible.

ClearTone is communication coaching support. It is not therapy, legal advice, medical advice, mental-health treatment, emergency support, or crisis support.

Data ClearTone Handles

Depending on how you use the app, ClearTone may handle message drafts, generated reply options, coaching guidance, saved local history, preferred replies, relationship briefs, account information, hosted AI usage, and subscription status connected to App Store purchases.

Local-First Analysis

ClearTone's local analysis runs on your device. Local drafts, local history, local feedback, local learning memory, and local WhatsApp import digests stay on your device unless you choose a feature that sends data to ClearTone's backend.

You can delete local device data from Settings.

WhatsApp Imports

ClearTone supports manual import of text-only WhatsApp exports. ClearTone does not connect to WhatsApp directly and does not read your WhatsApp account.

When you import a supported text export, ClearTone processes the text locally and creates a compact relationship brief. Voice notes, images, videos, stickers, and other media files are not analyzed in this release.

Hosted Coaching

Hosted deep coaching is an optional paid enhancement. It is only used when the app is configured for hosted AI, you are signed in, your account has an active entitlement, and you have remaining usage for the current period.

Before paid provider calls are approved, hosted provider calls remain disabled and the app falls back to local analysis.

Accounts, Subscriptions, And Deletion

If you create or use a ClearTone account, ClearTone may store account-scoped data such as an authenticated user identifier, hosted usage counters, paid-plan entitlement rows, synced AI feedback, and approved cloud memory derived from feedback.

ClearTone subscriptions are managed through Apple. Deleting your ClearTone account does not cancel App Store billing.

Safety

ClearTone may generate safety-oriented guidance when a message appears to involve threats, coercion, stalking, fear, or immediate danger. This guidance is not emergency support. If you are in immediate danger, contact local emergency services, crisis resources, or trusted support in your area.
