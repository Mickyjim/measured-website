Support

Help for ClearTone users.

This page gives users a public support route for app issues, WhatsApp imports, subscriptions, data deletion, and safety boundaries.

Contact

Launch support and legal contact details will be added before this page is used for App Store submission.

Until launch, this page explains the support scope and product boundaries.

What We Can Help With

  • App crashes or blank screens.
  • Trouble signing in.
  • Purchase or restore problems.
  • Account deletion questions.
  • Local data deletion questions.
  • WhatsApp text import problems.
  • Incorrect or confusing app behavior.
  • Accessibility, language, or privacy questions.

Subscriptions

ClearTone subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel billing, use Apple's subscription management. Deleting your ClearTone account does not cancel App Store billing.

Account And Data Deletion

ClearTone has two separate deletion paths: Delete Account & Cloud Data for account-scoped cloud data, and Delete All Device Data for local drafts, history, feedback, memory, WhatsApp import digests, and relationship briefs on that device.

WhatsApp Import Support

ClearTone supports manual import of text-only WhatsApp exports for the first release. ClearTone does not read WhatsApp directly. Voice notes, images, videos, stickers, and other media files are not analyzed in this release.

  • Export the chat without media.
  • Choose the text file when importing.
  • Use a shorter date range if the export is extremely large.
  • Keep the app open while the import is processing.

Safety

ClearTone is communication coaching support. It is not therapy, legal advice, medical advice, mental-health treatment, emergency support, or crisis support.

If you are in immediate danger, or if a situation involves threats, stalking, coercion, fear, or risk of harm, prioritize your safety and contact local emergency services, crisis resources, or trusted support in your area.
